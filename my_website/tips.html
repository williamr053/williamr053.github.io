Some Tips to Help You with your Coding

1) Always take your time when debugging, and don't get too frustrated.

2) Don't be afraid to ask for help if you are having trouble figuring out a problem.

3) Use your resources to find new ways to expand your coding knowledge.

4) Try to understand what causes the problem that is bothering you.

5) Make sure your grammar usage is always perfect.
